About Me

My name is Maria, I'm new to coding. I am a student at Denver University Coding Bootcamp program and work hard to learn coding basics. My background is in linguistics but I've always been interested in computers. I tried coding a little bit but was convinced that it is for boys (gender sterotypes are so strong in Russia). Now I'm older and understand that it is not about gender at all. It is what you can do and how you apply your knowledge. Coding is exiting!


As I already mentioned I'm originally from Russia so I love snow and skiing. Colorado is a perfect place for me. Before moving here we used to live in New York and Boston and those places are fun too. Me and my husband have a 7 years old daughter, a cat and fish. We love hiking, biking and movies.
